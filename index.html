About Us

METAL RESIDENT founded in Beijing in 2022.
We’re big into vintage clothing, cycling, and the outdoors — and we’ve always wanted gear that’s as stylish as it is functional. Chasing that idea, we hit the road to places where vintage and cycling culture are booming. Back home, we dug through fabric mills, and took off on long-distance rides to test ideas on the road.
Along the way, we pulled inspiration from all kinds of clothing traditions and tailoring techniques. Designs that mix timeless details with the needs of modern commuting, cycling, and outdoor living — built for comfort, style, and the ride.
